# Cozy Pom Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Cozy Pom respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

Cozy Pom does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

All data created within the app, including focus sessions, journal entries, focus quests, statistics, achievements, and settings, is stored locally on your device.

## Data Storage

Information entered into Cozy Pom remains on your device and is not uploaded to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

Cozy Pom does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or third-party tracking technologies.

## Data Sharing

Cozy Pom does not sell, share, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties.

## Children's Privacy

Cozy Pom does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
